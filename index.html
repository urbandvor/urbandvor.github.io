Marijin Dvor, Sarajevo

Central, connected, and easy to explore

Urban Dvor is located in one of Sarajevo's most convenient central neighborhoods, offering the perfect balance of city life and comfort. Within a short walk, you'll find UNITIC Business Center, Sarajevo City Center (SCC), Alta Shopping Center, Importanne Shopping Center, the National Museum of Bosnia and Herzegovina, cafés, restaurants, bakeries, supermarkets, pharmacies, and banks.

Whether you're visiting Sarajevo to explore its rich history, enjoy the local cuisine, or travel for business, everything you need is close at hand. The historic Baščaršija (Old Town) is about a 30-minute walk away or just a short tram ride away.
